News organizations first report on the call between Michael Flynn and Sergey Kislyak. David Ignatius of The Washington Post questions what Flynn said and wonders if the exchange (1) undercuts the U.S. sanctions against Russia, and (2) violates the spirit of the Logan Act, which bars U.S. citizens from correspondence intending to influence a foreign government about “disputes” with the United States.
