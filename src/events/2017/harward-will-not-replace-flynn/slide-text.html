Retired Vice Admiral Robert Harward declines Trump's offer to replace Michael Flynn as National Security Adviser after Flynn resigned.

Sources state that Harward declined the role due to financial and family considerations. Additionally, it is reported Trump insisted on retaining pro-Russia Deputy National Security Adviser K.T. McFarland. In 2013, McFarland made a statement that Putin should receive the Nobel Peace Prize. Harward, however, wanted to choose his own staff.
