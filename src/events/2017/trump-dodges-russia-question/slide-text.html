Trump did not respond to the final question of his first news conference since the convention when asked if he could affirmatively state that no one connected to him or his campaign had contact with Russia during the campaign. 

Ann Compton of ABC News asked: “Mr. President-elect, can you stand here today, once and for all, and say that no one connected to you or your campaign had any contact with Russia leading up to or during the presidential campaign?” Jim Acosta indicated that Trump answered "no" as he was leaving.

Source: NY Times
